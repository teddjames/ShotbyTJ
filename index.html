About Me

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, I am a seasoned photographer with five years of experience specializing in nature, street, urban, model, event, and automotive imagery. My work combines technical precision with refined aesthetics to tell compelling visual stories. While photography remains my primary focus, I also offer complementary videography skill

Equipment

I rely on my Nikon D5000 for its exceptional vintage image quality and ease of use. Its 12.3MP sensor and articulating LCD offer versatile performance for dynamic moments. I complement it with two lenses: a 15-300mm lens for a wide focal range—from expansive landscapes to detailed scenes—and an 18-55mm lens ideal for everyday, sharp, vibrant shots.

Experience

Over the past five years, I have passionately pursued photography, capturing the essence of fleeting moments and deep emotions at different notable events such as One Life Rally, Sunset GT and The Nairobi Fashion Pop-Up. My approach is both innovative and refined, transforming ordinary scenes into compelling visual narratives. Additionally, my exploration into videography has enhanced my storytelling, infusing my work with dynamic energy and a fresh perspective.

What I’m good at ?

Car Photography

I've dedicated my craft to capturing the sleek lines and raw power of automobiles at events like the Sunset Car Meet and One Life Rally, deepening my connection with a community that celebrates automotive art.

Sreet & Urban Photography

Years of experience in street and urban photography have taught me to capture the city’s soul—revealing the interplay of modern skyscrapers and historic facades, each image echoing urban energy and quiet elegance.

Nature Photography

Nature photography is an endless adventure. I've been capturing sunrise glows, majestic peaks, and quiet forest serenity—each shot a tribute to nature's timeless charm.

Model & Event Photography

Backed by extensive experience in this field. I've been capturing dynamic elegance at premier events, from runway shows to exclusive gatherings—each shot a tribute to individuality and timeless style.
